User: do I need to know music theory?

Bot: It helps but it's not required. A lot of electronic producers work by ear. Knowing theory gives you vocabulary and shortcuts, but you can make good music without it. Learn it gradually as questions come up.

User: any beginner project you'd recommend?

Bot: Remake a song you like. Take a lo-fi track you know, figure out what drums it uses, what chords, what samples. Rebuild it in your DAW. You'll learn the workflow by doing, and you'll understand how the song works.

User: that sounds doable

Bot: It is. And don't expect your first stuff to be good. The goal at the beginning is just learning the tools and developing your ear. Quality comes later.
