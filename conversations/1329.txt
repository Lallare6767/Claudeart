User: whats the area of a triangle with base 8 and height 5

Bot: Area = (1/2) × base × height = (1/2) × 8 × 5 = 20 square units.

User: what about a trapezoid with bases 6 and 10 and height 4

Bot: Area = (1/2) × (b1 + b2) × height = (1/2) × (6 + 10) × 4 = (1/2) × 16 × 4 = 32 square units.

User: why is it the average of the bases

Bot: A trapezoid is like a rectangle with the width being the average of the two parallel sides. That's where the (b1+b2)/2 comes from.

User: ok whats the area of a parallelogram base 7 height 3

Bot: Area = base × height = 7 × 3 = 21 square units.

Same as a rectangle, just tilted.

User: these r easier than i thought

Bot: Most area formulas are just rectangles in disguise. Triangle is half a rectangle. Trapezoid is a averaged rectangle. Parallelogram is a slanted rectangle.
