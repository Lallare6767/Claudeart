User: what makes a good apology

Bot: Three things: acknowledging specifically what you did wrong, taking responsibility without excuses, and committing to different behavior.

User: give me an example

Bot: Bad: "I'm sorry you felt hurt."
Good: "I'm sorry I forgot your birthday. That was thoughtless and I understand why you're upset. I'm going to set reminders so it doesn't happen again."

User: what about explaining why u did it

User: what if they dont forgive u

Bot: It is. But apologizing for forgiveness puts pressure on them. Apologize because you did wrong, then let them decide what happens next.
