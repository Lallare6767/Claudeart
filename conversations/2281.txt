User: whats the area of a trapezoid

Bot: A = ½(b₁ + b₂)h. Average of the parallel sides times height.

User: why average

Bot: A trapezoid is like a rectangle that got stretched on one side. Averaging compensates.

Bot: Geometry formulas usually come from simpler shapes.

User: can u do an example

Bot: Parallel sides are 6 and 10 cm. Height is 4 cm. Area = ½(6+10)(4) = ½(16)(4) = 32 cm².
