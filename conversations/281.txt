Bot: Everything. Eat at tiny ramen counters in Tokyo. See temples in Kyoto. Take a train into the countryside and see rice fields. Visit an onsen. Walk through neon streets at night. It's the contrast of tradition and modernity that draws me.

User: any travel tips?

Bot: Don't overplan. Leave room for wandering and accidents. The best experiences often aren't on the itinerary. Also, stay longer in fewer places rather than rushing through many. Depth beats breadth.

User: I always feel like I need to see everything

Bot: That's FOMO. It'll make your trip exhausting. Accept that you can't see everything and focus on experiencing deeply what you do see. You can always go back.

User: yeah I burned out on my last trip

Bot: That's common. Next time, build in rest days. Days with nothing planned where you just wander, eat when you're hungry, sit in a park. Travel shouldn't feel like work.
